Pricelists On Product With Variants

Overview

When using variants with multiple price lists it can be hard to keep track of the pricing of your items.

This module allows you to have a global view of all your pricing for an item and it's variants. It also lets you use the full detailled item price form directly from the product view so you don't have to go to the settings page to manage the pricing for the product or it's variants.

Features

Lists every products, variants and price lists for a given item in the pricing section of the product.

Allows the use of the full item price form directly from the product view.

Screenshots

Every price lists and variants are listed in the pricing.

When adding or editing an item, the full price list item form is displayed, giving you full control over the pricing without having to go to the configuration page.

To make use of this module you need to tick the following options under the user's technical settings: "Pricelists On Product", "Manage Pricelist Items" and "Sales Pricelists".
